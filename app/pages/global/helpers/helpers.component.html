Cookies

Goose uses cookies to help store and re-use data for other components options you have is settings, get and removing cookies .

This can be used as a call function like onclick etc and also be used to store / fetch cookies on page load, document ready as well. Storing a cookie, if you leave the expire date blank it will default to 365 days
